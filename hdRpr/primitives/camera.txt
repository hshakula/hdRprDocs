Supported:
* transform motion blur
* perspective and orthographic projections
* `focalLength`
* `focusDistance`
* `clippingRange`
* `fStop`
* `horizontalAperture`
* `verticalAperture`
* `shutterOpen`
* `shutterClose`
* `instantaneousShutter`

Limitations:
* `horizontalApertureOffset`
* `verticalApertureOffset`

More info about camera attributes: https://graphics.pixar.com/usd/docs/api/class_usd_geom_camera.htm
